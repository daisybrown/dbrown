Cafe bee

Porteath Bee Centre

About

  • Cafe bee is attractively positioned about 15 minutes walk from the SouthWest Coastal path near Polzeath and above the Porteath Bee centre. Bacon and eggs or coffee and croissants, light lunches or baguettes and bagels, Cream teas or a simple cuppa and cake. The choice is yours. Family and dog friendly. Plenty of board games to play and car parking is available!

Opening times

  • Monday - Sunday
  • 10am - 5pm
